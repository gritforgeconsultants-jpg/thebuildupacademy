Common Questions
Before You Register

Straight answers. No runaround.

Will this help if I'm a trade sub, not a GC?
Yes — and most seat holders in a typical cohort are subs. The curriculum is trade-agnostic. Scope language, exclusion templates, and sub quote review apply directly to steel, framing, mechanical, electrical, and specialty work.
Do I need estimating experience to attend?
Basic construction experience required — you should know what drawings look like and have submitted at least one bid. Not intro-level. Target participant: foreman or PM moving into estimating. Never been on a job site? This is not the right starting point.
What if I can't attend the live session?
The live session is where most of the value is — Module 6 particularly cannot be replicated in a recording. If you cannot commit to the date, hold your seat for the next cohort. The recording is a supplement, not a substitute.
Is the session recorded?
Yes. Private recording link within 24 hours of session end, with 30-day access. For review and reference — not a replacement for attending live.
Is this software training?
No software, no platforms, no demos. Pure estimating logic. The templates in your workbook adapt to whatever software or process your shop already uses.
What if the session date doesn't work once confirmed?
Email arron@gritforge.co before the date is announced and your seat transfers to the next cohort automatically. Once confirmed and within 7 days, seats are non-refundable — but the content guarantee stands regardless.
